About the Creators

The task management application was developed by a passionate team of Four members of CSE department dedicated to building intuitive and efficient productivity tools. With expertise in JavaScript Node.js, Express.js,and MongoDB, the team aimed to create a seamless and visually appealing Kanban-style platform that simplifies project tracking and task collaboration. Inspired by modern workflow methodologies like Agile and Scrum, the creators focused onfeatures such as drag-and-drop functionality, user authentication, and real-time task updates,ensuring the application caters to both individual users and teams. Whether you're a student, a professional, or part of a startup, the creators built thisapplication with the vision of helping users stay organized, manage time better, and achievegoals faster.
